About

Hi! My name is Federica Marini and I am from NYC. This is my first time making a personal website, and so over time it will probably change a lot. I will be using this space to publish series of photos that I have taken as well as videos that I have shot and edited. I hope that this space can serve as the foundation of my career in art x climate change activism.

Current Projects

My work spans across personal projects, event archivation, and academic research. My bachelor thesis explores the connection between Indigeneity and humans' relationship to the Earth as a way to understand the deepest roots of the climate crisis.
