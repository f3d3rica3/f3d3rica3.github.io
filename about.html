About

Hi! My name is Federica Marini and I am from NYC. This is my first time making a personal website, and so over time it will probably change a lot. I will be using this space to publish series of photos that I have taken, videos that I have shot and edited, and my research areas of interest. I studied economics and social sciences in my Bachelor and plan to specialize in environmental economics in my Master degree. I aspire to build a career as a climate change economist and art curator who creates exhibitions on the themes of intersectional environmentalism.
